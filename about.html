About Kyankima Women's Cooperative

Our History

Founded in 2019 in the Mukono District of Uganda, our cooperative began with 47 women. For years, we have grown our community through word-of-mouth and community radio[cite: 18]. Now, thanks to a UGX 12,000,000 grant, we are digitizing our operations to better serve our members and customers

Who We Are

We are a registered community-based organization involved in three main areas:

  • Small-Scale Agriculture: Farming local produce for the community[.
  • Handcraft Production: Creating unique products for a wider market.
  • Health Awareness: Partnering with 3 local health workers to provide monthly wellness tips.

Our Leadership

Under the guidance of our chairperson, Nalwoga Judith, we are committed to improving visibility and expanding our market reach across Uganda
